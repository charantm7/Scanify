# About Us

## We're building the future of restaurant ordering — one QR code at a time.

Scanify was born from a simple observation: too many restaurants were still handing out laminated paper menus, running to update printed price lists, and losing orders to miscommunication between tables and kitchens.

We decided to fix that.

---

## What Is Scanify?

Scanify is a QR-based digital menu and ordering platform designed specifically for restaurants, cafés, cloud kitchens, and food businesses across India.

With Scanify, your customers scan a QR code on their table and instantly get access to your full menu — complete with photos, descriptions, prices, and real-time availability. They can browse, order, and even pay, all from their own phone. No app download needed.

For restaurants, Scanify means fewer errors, faster service, lower printing costs, and a menu you can update in seconds — not days.

---

## What We Do

**For Restaurants:**

- Host and manage your digital menu from a simple dashboard
- Accept and track orders in real time
- Update item availability, prices, and descriptions instantly
- Get insights into what's selling and when

**For Customers:**

- Scan to browse a beautiful digital menu
- Place orders directly from the table
- Know exactly what you're getting — with photos and full descriptions
- No apps, no accounts required

## Why We Built Scanify

Eating out should be effortless. For too long, the experience has been broken on both sides of the table — customers flagging down staff to ask what's available, and restaurants struggling to keep menus accurate and orders organised.

We built Scanify to close that gap. To make the ordering experience as smooth for a busy restaurant on a Saturday night as it is for a quiet café on a Tuesday morning.

We are not a food delivery company. We are not an aggregator. We are a technology partner that sits quietly in the background and makes your restaurant run better.

## Our Values

**Simplicity first.**
If it takes more than a minute to set up or understand, we redesign it.

**Built for India.**
We understand Indian restaurants — the multilingual menus, the dynamic pricing, the GST requirements, the variety of service formats. Scanify is built with all of this in mind.

**Restaurants own their experience.**
We don't take a cut of your orders. We don't push ads on your customers. Your menu, your brand, your customers — Scanify just powers the technology.

**Reliability matters.**
When service is in full swing, downtime is not an option. We engineer for reliability first.
